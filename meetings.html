Attendance

Attending meetings is vital to supporting the club and its mission. If you miss two meetings in a row or throughout the year, you will be dropped from the club. We understand things happen that will prevent you from attending. Please see Ms. Strader to let her know you will not be attending (BEFORE the meeting) or it will count as an absence. If you sign up for an event and you find you cannot make it, it will count as an absence unless you find your own replacement. Please make every effort to attend. DO NOT have someone sign in for you. Since you were chosen for your integrity, responsibility and dependability, it is very important to portray these leadership qualities at all times, especially to your peers.
