Tips For

Ambassadors

At Student Services

How To Give A Great Tour

Make sure to utilize your time wisely and engage with the new student.

GRAB A MAP

Draw out their class route and go over the bell schedule.

KEEP IT CONCISE

Utilize time wisely, we want the student to get to class on time.

GO OVER SCHEDULE

Explain the block system, that they will attend the first 4 classes on top row of schedule (1st semester). Mention that homeroom doesn't occur regularly.

TOURING FORMAT

It is recommended that you follow the order of how their classes are listed.

INTRODUCE TO TEACHER

Make sure to introduce the student to their 1st period teacher before dropping them off.

RETURN TO STUDENT SERVICES

Don't linger in hallways after the tour, return to student services where you can be provided a pass to class if you are late.

Engage with the student. We don't want them to feel like they can't talk or ask questions. You are their first impression of the students at Cary High. We want it to be a good one.
